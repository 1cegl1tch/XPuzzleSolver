What is An X / Diamond Puzzle


X puzzles also knows as the diamond problem are most commenly used in algebra especially in trinomials
and quadratic equations.

If you look the problem you will notice that there are 4 spaces the top space the top space is for
the product, the bottom space for the sum, the left space for your first factor and the right space
space for your second factor. For instance you have the numbers 10 and 11, the ten is in the top space
and the eleven is in the bottom. So you need to find 2 numbers that when multiplied come out to 10 and when added
come out to 11.
